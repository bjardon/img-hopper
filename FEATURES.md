# Image Hopper

Image Hopper is a web-based client-only app for local image conversion. It supports (initially) HEIC to JPG/JPEG and PNG conversion, with more formats to come later. The app is free to use, no sign-ups required, and fully local — your images never leave your computer.

It is also open source! [Github](https://github.com/bjardon/image-hopper)
